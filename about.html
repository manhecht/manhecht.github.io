About Me

Creative technologist exploring the intersection of art, design, and technology.

Profile Image

Hello, I'm Manuel

I'm a creative technologist and full-stack developer specializing in interactive web applications, data visualization, and digital experiences. My work combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving to build innovative solutions at the intersection of technology and design.

With a strong background in both front-end and back-end development, I create responsive, user-friendly applications that deliver exceptional experiences. I'm particularly passionate about projects involving natural language processing, blockchain technology, and interactive data visualization.

I'm an associate lecturer for creative computing at UAS St. Pölten from 2025, where I share my knowledge and experience with the next generation of creative technologists.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge through workshops and community events.
